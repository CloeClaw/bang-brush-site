What's Your Face Shape?

Select the shape that best matches your face. Not sure? Use the guide below each option.

Oval

Forehead slightly wider than chin, with gentle curves. Face length is about 1.5x the width.

Round

Full cheeks, soft angles. Face width and length are nearly equal with a rounded hairline.

Oblong

Face is noticeably longer than wide. Forehead, cheeks, and jawline are similar width.

Heart

Wide forehead, prominent cheekbones, narrow chin that may come to a point.

Square

Strong, angular jawline. Forehead, cheekbones, and jaw are similar width.
