The Bang Method

Simple bang styling and a little overnight maintenance can help protect your hair from breakage and keep your look fresh for days. Here's how to get perfect, face-framing bangs.

1

Section & Sweep

Section out your bangs. Using the side bristles of the brush, gently sweep your bangs to one side while blow-drying. Continue until the roots to the mid-section are dry.

2

Switch Sides

Switch it up! Brush your bangs to the opposite side with the side bristles, following with the blow dryer. This sets the stage for soft, bouncy, face-framing bangs.

3

The Magic Move

Time for the magic move! Section your bangs horizontally. Place the brush under the section so the side rests lightly against your forehead. Blow air toward the center of the brush while brushing outward for that smooth, polished finish.

4

Overnight Care

For long-lasting style, try sleeping on a silk pillowcase or loosely wrapping your bangs. This helps prevent breakage, reduces frizz, and keeps your bangs looking salon-fresh for days.
